About Us

At Badger Bakery , we have been serving delicious baked goods to our community for 100 years. Our passion for baking started in 1923, and we are dedicated to using only the finest and freshest ingredients in our creations. From classic pastries to custom cakes,our baked goods are made from scratch daily and are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.

We are proud to be a part of this community and are grateful for the support of our customers over the years. So next time you're in the neighborhood, stop by Badger Bakery and taste the difference that love and passion make in every bite!
